Sponge Cake

ponge Chocolate cake is my most fav of all cakes.It has no butter in it so you can ,technically, eat as much as you want.

Ingredients:

  • Sugar - 2cups.
  • All-purpose flour - 13/4 cups.
  • Baking cocoa-3/4 cup.
  • Baking powder - 11/2 tsp.
  • Baking soda - 11/2tsp.
  • Salt - tsp.
  • Eggs - 2.
  • Milk - 1 cup.
  • Vegetable oil - 1/2 cup.(i use olive oil).
  • Boiling water - 1 cup.

Preparation:

  1. Heat oven to 350 F.
  2. Grease and flour two 9 inch round baking pans or one 13x9 inch pan.
  3. In large mixer bowl, stir together dry ingredients.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in boiling water by hand (batter will be thin).
  6. Pour into prepared pan.
  7. Bake 30 – 35 minutes . (Do not use 8-inch pans or the batter will overflow.).
  8. Cool 10 minutes. Remove from pan to wire racks.
  9. Delicious cake is ready...Enjoy...
