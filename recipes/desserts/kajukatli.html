Kaji Kati(Cashew cake)

Cashew cake is my Mom's recipe.Its a all time favorite for everyone.Just one bite & i feel that I'm in heaven.

Ingredients:

  • Cashew - 1 cup.
  • Milk - 1/4 cup.
  • Sugar - 1 cup.
  • Ghee - 4 spoons.
  • Cardamom - a pinch.

Preparations:

  1. Wash cashew with water.**Dont soak in water.
  2. Grind it with milk.
  3. In a heavy bottom pan,heat the ghee & wait for 30 sec.
  4. Put the cashew paste & cardamom powder in it .
  5. Stir it continuously till it stops sticking to the spatula.
  6. Put that in a large pan & allow it to cool for some time.
  7. After, cut into small pieces of your fav shape.
  8. Delicious Cashew cake is ready...Enjoy..
