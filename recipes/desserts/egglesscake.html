Eggless Cake

My mom is a huge fan of eggless cakes.When she visited us, I decided to surprise her by making this yummy condensed milk cake.I got this recipe from my aunt and we are all addicted to this cake.I used vanilla for flavor, you can use any flavor.Its totally up to you.You can also drizzle chocolate sauce over it.It makes a perfect tea time snack for my mom.

Ingredients:

  • Condensed milk - 1 can.
  • Sugar - 3 tbsp.
  • Salt - 1 tsp.
  • All purpose flour - 2 1/2 cups.
  • Baking powder - 1 tbsp.
  • Baking soda - 1 tbsp.
  • Lemon juice - 2 tbsp.
  • Vanilla extract - 1 tbsp.
  • Butter - 1 stick.

Preparation:

  1. Mix all the wet ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. In a large bowl , add all the dry ingredients without any lumps.Give a quick stir.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the large bowl and stir with a wooden spatula.
  4. In a greased pan,pour the mixture and bake in the preheated oven for 350”F for 25 – 30 min.
  5. Test it with a fork. It should come out clean.
  6. Remove the cake from oven and let it cool completely for 10 min.
  7. Delicious eggless cake is ready...Enjoy...
