Pearl Sago Payasam

Pearl sago(Javvarisi in Tamil) payasam is a traditional Indian recipe.For almost every celebrations in my home,my mom used to make this payasam for me.This payasam is my most favorite dessert of all.Love you mom

Ingredients:

  • Pearl Sago(Javvarisi) - 1 cup.
  • Milk - 1 cup.
  • Nuts of your choice - 2tbsp.
  • Cardamom powder - 1/4 tsp.
  • Sugar - 2-3 tbsp.

Preparation:

  1. Fry the nuts in ghee or butter and keep it aside.
  2. Again in the same pan ,fry the sago in ghee/butter Â till it becomes big (it turns light brown).
  3. Next ,pour 1 cup of water & let it boil nicely & completely.Add more water if necessary.
  4. When it is completely done,add milk & boil it.
  5. Finally add sugar.If you are serving for kids ,you can also add condensed milk.(it will give you the rich flavor.)
  6. Serve hot /cold of your choice & Decorate it with fried nuts.
  7. Delicious Sago payasam is ready...Enjoy...
