Vanilla Pound Cake

Pound cakes are my most favorite cakes of all.I made this vanilla pound cake and to my surprise my husband ate it all!!(fact), leaving me a single piece behind(so kind of him!!!).This cake has simple ingredients but a true feast for your taste buds.Try and enjoy it.

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour - 1 cup.
  • Sugar - 1/2 - 3/4 cup(depends on you).
  • Unsalted butter - 1 stick(1 cup -room temp).
  • Salt - 1 tsp.
  • Vanilla Extract - 2tsp.
  • Eggs - 2

Preparation:

  1. Beat butter and sugar until fluffy in a hand mixer.
  2. Add salt, vanilla extract and eggs.
  3. Beat until it all mixed well.
  4. Now slowly add the flour little by little till it all mixed nicely.
  5. Pour the batter in a greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake in a pre-heated oven for 350 F until you smell the cake.
  7. To make sure, stick a tooth pick.If it comes out clean,cake is just perfect.
  8. Cool it for 15 min.
  9. Delicious, yummy vanilla pound cake is ready...Enjoy!!!
