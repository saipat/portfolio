Sambhar Rice

Sambhar Rice is a traditional South Indian recipe.It is so awesome that you will definitely make it so many times.It is a perfect dish since it has lot of veggies in it.

Ingredients:

  • Thoor Dhal - 3/4 cup.(Boil it in a pressure cooker with little oil & turmeric powder).
  • Boiled Rice(depends) - 2 cups.
  • Tamarind - a cube(soak it in hot water to get fine juice).
  • Coconut - 3/4 cup.
  • Coriander seeds - 1tsp.
  • Fenugreek seeds - 3/4 tsp.
  • Red Chili - 5.
  • Ghee.
  • Potato-1(diced).
  • Carrots -1 (diced).
  • Peas - 1/2 cup(boiled).
  • Tomato - 1(chopped).
  • Green Chili - 2
  • Onion - 1(Chopped).
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp.
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp.
  • Red chili powder - 1tsp.
  • Coriander leaves - finely chopped.
  • Curry leaves.
  • Hing - a pinch.
  • Oil, salt - as needed.

Preparation:

  1. For Masala: Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Add coriander seeds,fenugreek seeds,red chilies & coconut (one at a time) & fry it till nice aroma comes.
  3. Cool it & grind it into a fine paste.Keep it aside.
  4. Heat oil in another pan.
  5. Add mustard seeds & curry leaves.
  6. Now add the green chilies & onion.
  7. When it turns to golden brown, add the tamarind juice.
  8. Let it boil for 2 min.Now add all the veggies,tomatoes,salt, turmeric powder & hing.
  9. Wait till the veggies get tender .It may take 10min.
  10. Now add the masala.Mix it well.
  11. Add red chili powder.
  12. Now add the boiled thoor dhal.
  13. Let it boil with the veggies.Just wait for 2 min.
  14. Now add rice & coriander leaves.
  15. Serve hot with pappads.
  16. Delicious Sambhar rice is ready...Enjoy with your family.
