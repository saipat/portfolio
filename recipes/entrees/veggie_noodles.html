Veggie Noodles

Veggie noodles are my all time favorite dish.This is my Bff's version of veggie noodles.Thanks for this yummy recipe annie.

Ingredients:

  • Bell peppers - 1 cup(any color,thinly sliced).
  • Green bell pepper - 1/2 cup(thinly sliced).
  • Carrots - 1(thinly sliced).
  • Cabbage - 1/2 cup.(thinly grated).
  • Onion - 1/2 cup(finely chopped) .
  • Tomato - 1/2.(chopped).
  • Maggi hot and sweet sauce - 2 tbsp.
  • Black pepper and salt to taste.
  • Thin spagetti noodles(as needed).

Preparation:

  1. Saute all the veggies till the are cooked.
  2. Boil the noodles with salt.
  3. Mix the veggies and the noodles.
  4. Delicious noodles is ready...Enjoy!!!
