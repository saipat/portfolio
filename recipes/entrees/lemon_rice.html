Lemon Rice

Lemon Rice is famous,traditional South Indian Recipe.This is actually my grandma's recipe.I remember that all my friends were  a huge fan of this recipe.Believe me,it is DIVINE.....

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice - 2 1/2 cups.
  • Channa dhal - 1tsp.
  • Curry Leaves - 5-6.
  • Mustard seeds - 1tsp.
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2tsp.
  • Hing - a pinch.
  • Salt to taste.

Preparation:

  1. Extract the juice from 1 lemon & keep it aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Fry mustard seeds,curry leaves,grad dhal,green chili & channa dhal.
  4. After 1 min,keep it in simmer.Now add the lemon juice.
  5. After 30 secs,add 1/4  cup water.
  6. Now add turmeric powder,hint & salt.
  7. Cook for 2mins.
  8. Mix it with cooked rice & garnish it with coriander leaves.
  9. (Add the roasted peanuts & cashews if you want.).
  10. Serve it with garlic pickle or potato fry.Potato fry is our favorite combination.
  11. Delicious Lemon rice is ready...Enjoy...
