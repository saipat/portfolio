Paneer Roll

Paneer chapathi rolls is a very tasty recipe.It is very easy to make.I bet kids will love it.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer - 2 cups(Scramble the paneer cubes).
  • Tomatoe - 2(chopped finely).
  • Chat masala - 1tsp.
  • Garam masala - 1tsp.
  • Red chili powder - 1tsp.
  • Turmeric powder - 1 tsp.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Coriander (finely chopped).
  • Green chili - 2(finely chopped).
  • Curd - 1tsp.
  • Wheat flour - 2 cups.
  • Milk - as Knead to need the dough.

Preparation:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the tomatoes,paneer,chat masala,gram masala,red chili powder,turmeric powder,salt coriander,Green chilies & curd.
  2. Leave the mixture for 10 – 15 min.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Fry the mixture for 5 – 10 min till its done..Keep it aside.
  4. In a separate bowl, pour the hot milk & add the wheat flour.(add sugar if you like it.I added it.)
  5. Knead the dough softly.
  6. Make the chapathi as you normally do.
  7. Place the mixture on the chapathi and roll it.
  8. Delicious Paneer chapathi rolls are ready...Enjoy!!!
