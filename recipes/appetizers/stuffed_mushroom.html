Stuffed Mushrooms

There is no word to describe the awesomeness of the stuffed mushrooms.I first ate it in Olive Garden and i am addicted to it.This recipe is my version of stuffed mushroom with feta cheese and red bell peppers.It is very easy to prepare and a good appetizer for large crowds.

Ingredients:

  • Any mushroom of your choice .
  • Red bell peppers - 1/2(finely chopped).
  • Mint leaves - 3 – 4(finely chopped).
  • Egg plant - 1/2(slice thinly).
  • Bread scrums .
  • Feta cheese.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Preparation:

  1. Scoop out the inside part of the mushrooms.
  2. Place the thinly sliced egg plant in a pan.
  3. Place the mushroom on top of it.
  4. Stuff inside with feta cheese,red bell pepper,bread scrums.
  5. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  6. You can also squeeze lemon juice on top of it.
  7. Place in the preheated oven for 400 F for 30 min or until it becomes golden brown.
  8. Delicious Stuffed mushroom is ready...Enjoy!!!
