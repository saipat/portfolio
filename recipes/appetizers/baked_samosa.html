Vegetable Baked Samosa:

Samosas are everyone's favorite in my family.They are so yummy and spicy that you cannot stop with just one.That too,when you add magi's hot and spicy ketchup, it is heaven right there in your plate.This recipe is my aunt's with a twist of my own.She always fry them and i thought to try them differently (and to make it healthy too..)Guess what that is....BAKING!!! It turned out even yummier than the fried version.

Ingredients:

  • All purpose flour - 2 cups.
  • Ajwain seeds - 2 tsp.
  • Baking powder - 1tbsp.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Olive oil - 3tbsp.
  • Water to make dough.
  • For stuffing:
  • Veggies of your choice  - 1 cup.
  • Onion - 1/2 cup(chopped finely).
  • Tomato - 1/2(chopped finely).
  • Mint leaves - 3(chopped ).
  • Coriander leaves (chopped).
  • Ginger-galic paste - 1 tbsp.
  • Turmeric powder - 1tsp.
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp.
  • Chili powder - 1 tbsp.
  • Garam masala powder - 2tsp.
  • Salt to taste.

Preparation:

  1. Mix the first 5 ingredients and make a dough.Cover it with wet towel and set aside for few min.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.Fry onions until translucent.
  3. Add g-g paste and fry for a min.now add all powders with salt and tomato.
  4. Fry for 3-5 min with little splash of water.
  5. Now add all the veggies and cook for about 7-10 min and set aside and let it cool.
  6. Now make small balls out of the dough.
  7. Flatten it and cut it into half.(semicircle).
  8. Now make it like a cone and stuff the masala inside and close it nicely(Use little water to close the ends).
  9. Bake in the oven for 400 degree F  for about 30 - 35 min are until golden brown.
  10. Delicious, spicy samosas are ready...Enjoy!!!
