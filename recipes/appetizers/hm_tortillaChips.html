Home-made Tortilla Chips:

Tortilla chips is everyone's favorite mexican appetizer.I bought tortilla for making burrito and the leftover tortillas were sitting in the fridge for a while.So, I decided to make chips.I made two kinds of salsa for the dipping and both of them were a big hit in my family.

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas - 2.
  • Sea salt for seasoning.(you can add any seasoning you want).
  • Olive oil - 1tbsp.

Preparation:

  1. Cut the tortillas into small pieces.
  2. Pour the oil and sprinkle the seasoning of your choice.
  3. Mix them well with your hands.
  4. Bake them in a preheated oven for 350 F for 15 – 10 min.
  5. Delicious Tortilla chips are ready...Enjoy!!!
