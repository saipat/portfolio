Mushroom Manchurian

My Daughter loves mushroom very much.She eats all kind of mushrooms and I'm finding new ways to make it more interesting to her.My husband suggested to make mushroom manchurian and i thought, "oh gosh i never made it before.". Guess what? it turned out so awesome that she started asking for more even though it was little spicy for her age.(she's going to be 1 soon...hurray!!!).

Ingredients:

  • Mushroom of your choice - 5 (i used baby bella mushroom.chop it into thin slices).
  • All purpose flour - 4 tbsp.
  • Corn flour - 2tbsp.
  • Gram flour - 3 tbsp.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Chili powder - 1tbsp.
  • Turmeric powder - 1tsp.
  • Chili paste - 2tsp.
  • Scallions - 3(chopped).
  • Onions - 1 cup(chopped).
  • Tomato - 1/2(chopped).
  • Mint leaves - 2.
  • Coriander leaves(chopped).
  • Green bell pepper - 1/2(chopped).
  • Red bel pepper - 1/2(chopped).

Preparation:

  1. Mix all the flours with salt, chili powder and little water to make paste.
  2. Dip mushroom slices one by one and deep fry them.
  3. Transfer then to a paper towel and let it there for few min.
  4. Heat oil in another pan.Fry onions and bell peppers for 5 min.
  5. Add tomato and all the powders with salt.
  6. Fry then for another 5 min.
  7. Now add the chili paste and give it a mix.
  8. Add the fried mushrooms alone with the chopped scallions .
  9. Mix them well and cook for 3-5 min.
  10. Delicious , Tasty mushroom manchurian is ready...Enjoy!!!
